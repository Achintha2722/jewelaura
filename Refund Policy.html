Refund policy


Eligibility:

You may request a refund or exchange within 7 days of the purchase date. To be eligible, the item must be unused, unworn, and in the same condition as received, with all original tags and packaging.

Refund Process:

Contact our customer service team to initiate a return or exchange. We'll provide you with a return authorization and instructions.

Quality Inspection:

All returns will undergo a thorough quality inspection. If the item does not meet our return policy criteria, we reserve the right to refuse the return or charge a restocking fee.

Exchanges:

If you wish to exchange an item, please indicate the new item you'd like. If the new item costs more, the additional payment will be required. We'll process the exchange upon receiving the returned item.

Damaged or Defective Items:

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately for assistance. We will arrange a return or replacement at no extra cost.
